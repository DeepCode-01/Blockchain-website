Our Services

Blockchain development services encompass a wide range of offerings that cater to the specific needs of businesses and individuals looking to leverage blockchain technology. Here's a breakdown of some of the most common services included

Blockchain Platform Development

Some companies offer services to develop custom blockchain platforms tailored to the specific needs of their clients. This can be a good option for businesses that require a high degree of customization and control over their blockchain infrastructure.

Smart Contract Development

Smart contracts are self-executing contracts written in code that are stored and executed on a blockchain. They can automate agreements and processes, eliminating the need for intermediaries and reducing the risk of fraud. Blockchain development services can help businesses design, develop, and deploy secure and reliable smart contracts.

Decentralized Application (dApp) Development

dApps are applications built on top of a blockchain network that operate in a decentralized manner. They are not controlled by any single entity and offer advantages such as transparency, security, and censorship resistance. Blockchain development services can help businesses build and launch dApps for a variety of purposes, such as supply chain management, financial services, and social networking.

Blockchain Consulting

This service involves helping clients understand blockchain technology and how it can be applied to their specific business goals. Consultants can provide guidance on the feasibility of blockchain projects, identify potential use cases, and recommend the most suitable blockchain platforms and tools.

Tokenization

Tokenization is the process of converting real-world assets into digital tokens that can be traded on a blockchain. Blockchain development services can help businesses tokenize assets such as securities, real estate, and intellectual property.

Security Audits

Blockchain technology is still relatively new and evolving, and there are security risks associated with it. Blockchain development services can help businesses identify and mitigate these risks by conducting security audits of their blockchain applications and infrastructure.

Integration Services

Blockchain applications often need to be integrated with existing systems and infrastructure. Blockchain development services can help businesses with this process, ensuring that their blockchain applications are seamlessly integrated with their other systems.

Maintenance and Support

Once a blockchain application is launched, it needs ongoing maintenance and support. Blockchain development services can provide businesses with the expertise and resources they need to keep their applications running smoothly and securely.

FAQ

What is an admin and dashboard template?

It is a Front-end kit with prebuilt UI components for the creation of the interface for the web apps. It contains HTML, CSS, and JavaScript code. You can use it to create an admin console and dashboards.

Why do I need EasyDev?

Using templates saves you time and money because you don’t need to design and code the front-end part from scratch. So it allows you to get up and run your project faster and easier. Also, it contains a few useful ready-to-use integrations and apps. You can use them as an example to build your own or implement them directly into your project. EasyDev has a good structure and code hence fits well for learning purposes.

What skills do I need to have to use EasyDev?

EasyDev is a React-based template. To use it efficiently general knowledge of Front-end development is required. You might need more in-depth skills in React to customize components. Also, keep in mind that the template is not contains the back-end. To make an end-product you need to have knowledge or help with the back-end part.

Do you provide any design frames?

Yes. You will find the link to the Figma frames. You can copy the frames, and customize them as you want in your Figma-project.

Is this landing page a part of the EasyDev template?

Yes. And you are free to use it. However, please remember, if you use it for commercial products you need to buy an Extended license.

What’s the difference between regular and extended licenses?

The regular license can be used for non-commercial end products (access is free and there will be no monthly subscription fee). You need to buy a new single regular license for each product you make or each domain you use if you set up it on multiple domains. An extended license can be used for end products (web service or SaaS) that charge users for access or service (e.g: monthly subscription fee). For more info on Themeforest licenses, you can check http://themeforest.net/licenses/standard?license=regular.
